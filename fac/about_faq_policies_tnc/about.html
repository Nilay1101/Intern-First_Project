About us

Have you ever thought of being a sports person? Do you think you can contribute towards the development of sports in India? Do you fell you want to be always connected to the sports you really like? You always wanted to meet new players from your town with whom you can play and learn? Have you ever thought of being a part of the change which you want to see in Indian sports? If answer to any one of the above is Yes, then you are at the right place. TheSportz.com helps everyone to connect, play, share, learn and do a lot more about their favourite sports in a whole new way.

We started this to achieve the biggest dream of every sports enthusiast in India. The dream of becoming the best in every sport which is played around the world. The passion which our team has for sports and it's development makes us work through a lot of sleepless nights to develop a hassle free and efficient sports ecosystem. Our idea is to develop a comprehensible, meticulous and opento-all online platform where you can find all the information related to your favourite sport which enables every individual to achieve more.

We envisage a future where every Indian sports aspirant would have the knowledge about how to proceed with a career in sports throughout his/her life. Every sports aspirant and enthusiast is connected to each other in a single platform, where they can share news, views, events, recommendations, sports blogs and testimonials, photographs, videos and many more. This would ultimately mean all things under one roof. One-stop solution for all your sporting needs.

Our ultimate aim is to create a transparent platform for everyone so that everyone can share, learn and grow together. Our collaboration with different sports fraternities and government bodies would mean professionalism and focus towards sports development in our work. Building sports communities in India which can interact with each other and share sports information seamlessly across all digital platforms will be our top priority always. Lets join hands to create such a platform for all. We believe sports is a fundamental right, just as education. Lets create a better future, start a whole new sports life " here "if you haven't done it yet!
